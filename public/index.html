About Aaradhya

Aaradhya Soni is a 12-year-old student currently in the 7th grade.

She began her inspiring journey with the Bhagavad Gita after seeing her 'Badi Mummy' (elder aunt), who was also a dedicated student.

Following in her aunt's footsteps, Aaradhya enrolled in classes with LearnGeeta and started her own journey of learning and recitation.
